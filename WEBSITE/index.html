Welcome to San Diego

Explore California's beach city

Learn more about California's finest city

San Diego has earned the title of California's beach city because of its near-perfect year-round weather, 70 miles of gorgeous coastline, world-class attractions, and a dynamic urban core with a sophisticated art, food, and nightlife culture. Explore the different coastal, urban, and inland communities of San Diego, each with its own distinct flavor. Don't miss out on trying the region's characteristic Cali Baja cuisine or one of the region's hundreds of specialty beers and wines. After a day at the beach or other outdoor excursion, head to one of the city's hottest nightclubs or rooftop bars to party. If art and culture are your passions, San Diego is a vibrant center of creativity.San Diego is calling your name; heed the call and begin organizing your vacation right away!.
